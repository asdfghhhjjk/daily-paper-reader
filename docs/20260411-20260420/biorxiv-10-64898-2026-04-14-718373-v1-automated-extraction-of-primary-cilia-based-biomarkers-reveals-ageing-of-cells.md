## 摘要
我们提出了一种自动化图像分析方法，用于定量评估培养的人原代成纤维细胞中的初级纤毛。所提出的方法实现了一个模块化处理流程，将基于深度学习的细胞核分割与基于强度驱动的纤毛轴突及基体分割相结合。这些局部分割结果通过基于距离的关联策略整合，从而实现单个纤毛的自动重建及后续几何特征的提取。我们将其性能与人工分割的真实标注进行对比评估。尽管人工标注系统性地低估了纤毛的长度，但自动化和人工分析在群体层面趋势一致，并在不同样本组间表现出相同的统计区分能力。该流程的应用揭示了随着传代次数增加，纤毛长度和纤毛化率的逐渐降低，表明该方法能够敏感地检测微妙的形态变化。所提出的框架可实现可扩展、可重复且客观的初级纤毛形态定量分析，并为细胞衰老及相关表型的高通量研究提供了计算工具。

与本研究相关的所有代码已在 GitHub 上公开：https://github.com/reyesaldasoro/Cilia。

## Abstract
We present an automated image-analysis methodology for quantitative assessment of primary cilia in cultured human primary fibroblasts. The proposed approach implements a modular processing pipeline combining deep-learning based nuclear segmentation with intensity-driven segmentation of the ciliary axoneme and basal body. These partial segmentations are integrated using a distance-based association strategy, enabling automated reconstruction of individual cilia and subsequent extraction of geometrical features. Performance was evaluated against manually segmented ground truth. While manual annotations showed a systematic underestimation of cilia length, both automated and manual analyses produced consistent population-level trends and identical statistical discrimination across sample groups. Application of the pipeline revealed a progressive reduction in cilia length and ciliation frequency with increasing passage number, demonstrating the sensitivity of the method to subtle morphological changes. The proposed framework enables scalable, reproducible, and objective quantification of primary cilia morphology and provides a computational tool applicable to high-throughput studies of cellular ageing and related phenotypes.

All the code related to this work is available through GitHub: https://github.com/reyesaldasoro/Cilia.
